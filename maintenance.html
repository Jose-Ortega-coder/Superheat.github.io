Maintenance Plans & HVAC Tips

Why Maintenance Matters

Regular HVAC maintenance helps prevent expensive repairs, improves efficiency, and keeps your home safe during extreme Chicago weather. Our yearly plans typically include one cooling check and one heating check.

During these visits, our team cleans important components, checks for early warning signs, and makes sure your system is operating safely.

Simple HVAC Tips

  • Change your air filter every 1–3 months.
  • Keep vents and returns unblocked and free of dust.
  • Schedule tune-ups before summer and before winter.
  • Pay attention to new noises, odors, or uneven temperatures.

Small steps like these can save money over time and reduce the chance of your system breaking down when you need it most.

Routine inspections catch small issues before they turn into major repairs.
