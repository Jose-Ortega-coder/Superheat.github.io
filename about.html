About SuperHeat and SuperCool

SuperHeat and SuperCool is a locally operated HVAC company based in Chicago, Illinois. We focus on helping homeowners and small businesses stay comfortable through every season, from freezing winters to hot summers.

Our team believes in honest recommendations, clear explanations, and quality work. We explain what’s going on with your system in simple terms so you can make informed decisions without feeling pressured.

What We Stand For

  • Transparent, up-front pricing
  • Respect for your home and schedule
  • Showing you what we’re doing and why
  • Providing reliable, long-term solutions

Our service vehicle is stocked with common parts and tools so many repairs can be completed in a single visit.
